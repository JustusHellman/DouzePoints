Cookie Policy

01 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help the site work and improve your experience.

02 Types we use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (no tracking).

  • Performance/Analytics: Counts visits and improves the site.

  • Advertising cookies: Used to show ads and measure ad performance. These can be personalized.

03 Your choices

  • Accept all: Allows all cookies (including personalized ads).

  • Use essential only: Blocks advertising/personalization cookies.

You can change your choice by clearing site data or using the Manage button in the footer.

04 Third parties

We work with advertising partners (e.g., Google) who may set cookies. We use Google Consent Mode to control personalization.
